In-game description

An unidentified Digimon that suddenly appeared on a computer network. A single DigiEgg was born from the malice of humans abusing computer networks and the aggression from conflicts unfolding over networks. The DigiEgg was the amalgamation of humanity's destructive instincts, and the mysterious Digimon born from it is incredibly dangerous. It multiplies within a computer network like a virus, causing minor network failures.
